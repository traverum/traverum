# Guest (Traveler)

## Where this lives
- **App:** Widget (`apps/widget`)
- **Routes:** `/{hotelSlug}/*` (hotel channel), `/experiences/*` (Veyond direct)
- **Key code:** `apps/widget/src/app/[hotelSlug]/`

## Who is this person?

Tourists and travelers looking for local experiences — wine tastings, guided tours, boat trips, equipment rentals. They arrive through a hotel's website (embedded widget) or directly through Veyond's booking platform. They may not be tech-savvy and are often browsing on mobile.

## What do we promise them?

Trusted, beautiful, effortless booking. Discover experiences curated by their hotel or by Veyond. See clear pricing, pick a time, enter details, pay. Done. If they request a custom time, they get a response within 48 hours. They always know what's happening — clear status, clear next steps.

## Success looks like

- Books an experience in under 3 minutes from first click to payment
- Never confused about what happens next
- Feels like the hotel's own experience program, not a third-party marketplace

## What should never happen

- Pays but the supplier doesn't know about it
- Left wondering what's happening with their request
- Sees a broken or foreign-looking widget on the hotel's website
- Encounters hidden fees or surprise charges at checkout
