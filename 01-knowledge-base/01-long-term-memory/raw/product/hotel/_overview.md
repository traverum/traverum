# Hotel (Distribution Partner)

## Where this lives
- **App:** Dashboard (`apps/dashboard`)
- **Routes:** `/hotel/*`
- **Key code:** `apps/dashboard/src/pages/hotel/`

## Who is this person?

Hotel owners, general managers, or marketing managers. They want to enhance their guest experience and earn additional revenue, but they have zero time for operations. They are not in the business of running tours — they just want to offer them.

## What do we promise them?

Zero effort. Embed a widget once, toggle experiences on/off, earn commission. The widget looks like it belongs to the hotel — their brand, their colors, their fonts. No booking management, no customer service, no operational overhead. Passive income from guest bookings.

## Success looks like

- Widget embedded in under 5 minutes (copy-paste a code snippet)
- Experiences appear that are relevant to their location (automatic, distance-based)
- Earns commission without any daily involvement
- Guests perceive the experiences as curated by the hotel, not a third party

## What should never happen

- Widget looks foreign or breaks the hotel's website
- Hotel needs to manage bookings or handle guest issues
- Irrelevant experiences appear (wrong location, wrong type)
- Setup requires technical knowledge beyond copy-paste
