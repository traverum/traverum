# Platform (Traverum Admin)

## Where this lives
- **App:** Admin (`apps/admin`)
- **Routes:** `/admin/*`
- **Key code:** `apps/admin/src/pages/`

## Who is this person?

The Traverum team. Internal operators who manage the platform: onboarding partners, monitoring bookings, handling payouts, resolving disputes, and using "act as partner" to support suppliers and hotels.

## What do we promise them?

Full visibility and control over the platform. See all partners, all bookings, all payouts. Act as any partner to debug issues or provide support. Manage hotel payouts and supplier settlements. Monitor system health.

## Success looks like

- Can onboard a new partner in under 5 minutes
- Can investigate and resolve a booking issue without touching the database
- Can see platform-wide revenue and booking volume at a glance
- "Act as partner" lets them see exactly what a supplier or hotel sees

## What should never happen

- A payout fails silently without anyone noticing
- A partner is stuck because admin can't see their view
- Platform operations require direct database access for routine tasks
